Friends of Urban Plots

Friends of Urban Plots explores how we approach cities when we have access to the information of all cities around the globe.

Friends of Urban Plots creates a digital panorama of urban plots from the geometric data collected from the OpenStreetMap. The digital panorama is a clustering of urban plots that are encoded by shooting rays from the buildings of the plot to their surroundings, resulting a smooth-changing pattern of typical urban building layouts. Cities are indexed by the distribution of their plots over the obtained panorama. The indexes reveal the different characteristics of the adjacent neighborhoods in the same city, or the same characteristic shared by multiple neighborhoods across different cities.
